Water Sports & Activities

Fishing

Grab your reels and pack your bait! Pinnacle Mountain State Park and its surroundings offer access to several bodies of water. Lake Maumelle, the Big and Little Maumelle Rivers, and the Arkansas River all offer anglers and fishermen prime fishing spots to fish the day way. Some of the fish you'd likely encounter are the largemouth and spotted bass. Catfish also make an appearance in these waters and is recommended to bait them with worms, minnows and stink baits.

Paddle Sports

Fishing's not the only thing you can expect from the waters of Arkansas. Enjoy the beauty of the Arkansan landscape by canoe or kayak. White-water rafting to a leisurely float, Pinnacle Mountain State Park offers plenty of experiences to be had on the water.

Before embarking on any naval adventures, please be sure to follow the following advice and tips:

  1. Wear your personal flotation device
  2. Know the basic paddling techniques
  3. Be aware of the water conditions
  4. Dress for the water, not for the air
  5. Know your limits
  6. Be aware of your trip
  7. Practice self-rescue techniques
  8. Be aware of hidden hazards
  9. Treat the environment as your home
