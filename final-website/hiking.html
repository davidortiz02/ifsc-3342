Pinnacle Mountain State Trails

Hiking and Biking at Pinnacle Mountain State Park

Whether you're looking for a pleasant stroll through the park or looking for something challenging and fulfilling, Pinnacle Mountain State Park is home to a multitude of trails. From easy day hikes and bike trails well suited to beginners, to more difficult trails fitting for those wanting something more, Pinnacle Mountain State Park is your #1 stop for all your outdoor needs. Be sure to plan accordingly and to the best of your abilities.
